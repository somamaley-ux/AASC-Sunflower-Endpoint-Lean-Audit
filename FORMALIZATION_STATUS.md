## Current Result

Lean proves, without a governance or population premise, that every initial
minimal-blocker coordinate has a finite blocker-incidence path to a literal
terminal blocker coordinate, and it carries the generated package through

This combinatorial construction remains the foreground mathematics. Kernel

crosses, collisions, certificates, or numerical bounds; its kernel-faithful
exhaustion and impossibility consequences close the terminal structural
alternatives that combinatorics has generated.

The checked dependency is now:

```text
minimal blocker and private residual
  -> nonempty residual/next-blocker intersection

  -> reduction minimality gives certificate terminality

  -> support-fibre and minimal-blocker bounds
  -> coverage rigidity and downstream source injection

```

The path-population and residual-parent/slot arrows are unconditional finite
combinatorics. Lean now proves that every distinct raw private witness changes
complete tensor content and that all same-parent multiplicity is exhausted by
a slot in a fibre of cardinality less than `k`. It also proves that arbitrary
residual collisions need not be injective: they are locally bounded, separated,
sunflower-producing, or assigned a strict lower-rank decomposition candidate.
The global component code is injective on residual edges, but this is ordinary
set reconstruction and is not a root-to-terminal selector. The graded-product
and weighted-charge modules are non-load-bearing support for a distinct
autonomous conventional strengthening. The checked Hall/current-reference
equivalences document why raw injectivity cannot substitute for the integrated
disposition-and-closeout theorem.

## Autonomous Conventional Strengthening

`RankCompatibleProductGate.lean` shows that the older full-product versus
literal-missing-tuple split was too coarse. A missing tuple can fail merely
because its component ranks do not add to `r + 1`. Indeed, when the second
matching has two petals, the tuple taking both whole petals has rank
`2 * (r + 1)`, so unrestricted product fullness is impossible. Such a tuple is
not a useful compatibility obstruction.

The corrected exhaustive ledger is:

1. unrestricted product fullness;
2. fullness of exactly the rank-compatible layer, with only wrong-rank tuples
   absent; or
3. a genuinely missing rank-compatible tuple.

component slice sunflower-free and recursively bounded. Every occupied rank

The theorem in this exploratory module stops at the explicit finite

distinct autonomous single-language theorem, not the integrated proof.
